Campus Ambassador (CA) Program

Become a pivotal part of our entrepreneurial community as a Campus Ambassador (CA). As a CA, you will:

  • Promote E-Cell initiatives and events within your network.
  • Act as a liaison between E-Cell and students.
  • Gain exclusive access to workshops and mentoring sessions.
  • Receive a certificate of recognition and other incentives.

Join us and be the face of innovation and entrepreneurship at Sharda University!
